Guarantees

Four things we do. Four things we don't.

We store per-session

Every session is its own Durable Object. Data lives there until deletion. No cross-session joins, ever.

We log every consent choice

Each participant's visibility pick is timestamped. Exports include the log. Auditable by a works council.

We gate results by tally

A result is hidden until the minimum vote count is met. Default 5. No single-voter exposure, anywhere.

We delete on schedule

Identity rows purge on the retention you pick. Aggregate tallies can live longer — without link back.

We don't sell data

No ads network, no data broker, no telemetry pipeline to a third party. Your tallies are yours.

We don't train on you

Nothing in Qesto feeds a training run — not ours, not a vendor's. Workers AI is used for inference, never fine-tuning.

We don't route to third-party AI

No OpenAI, no Anthropic, no Azure. Inference stays on Cloudflare's network, in your region.

We don't resolve identity late

Once a vote is anonymous, it stays anonymous. We can't un-anonymize — not for subpoena, not for us.

Data lifecycle

Where your data lives, and for how long.

During the session

In the Durable Object

Votes, tallies, and consent choices live in a single DO, pinned to the colo closest to first-mover. No replication.

After close

In your workspace

Session locks. Recap generates. Data moves to R2 (object storage) for retention window. Encrypted at rest.

Retention end

Purged by scheduler

Cron job on every DO sweeps expired sessions. Identity rows go first; aggregate tallies follow on your schedule.
