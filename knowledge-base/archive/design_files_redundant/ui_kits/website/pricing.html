Pricing questions

Answers to what you're about to ask.

What counts as a "session"?

A session is one room — one join code, one set of participants, one retention window. Questions inside that room are free. Rooms don't expire until you close them or retention kicks in.

Do participants pay?

Never. Qesto charges the host only. An audience of 5 and an audience of 5,000 cost the same.

Can I cancel?

Yes, any time, from the billing page. Monthly cancels immediately with no refund on the current month. Annual cancels at renewal.

What if my first pulse flops?

If your first session doesn't beat the response rate of your last survey, email us within 14 days of your first billing. We'll refund the full quarter.

How does usage-based billing work on Chorus?

It doesn't. Chorus is a flat annual. No per-session, per-seat, or per-response surprises.

Is there a free tier for students?

Yes — Pulse is free forever, no cap on participants. For coursework at scale (> 3 concurrent rooms), we grant free Signal access via the .edu program.
