## Body (short — blog / email)

Qesto **7.0** ships today as the **Engagement Intelligence Network** — a major step beyond live polling into cross-session analytics, multi-org federation, and supervised AI facilitation.

**What's new in 7.0:**

- **PULSE** — people-ops engagement analytics with k-anonymity and GDPR-aware retention
- **COPILOT** — live AI facilitator co-pilot with human-in-the-loop checkpoints (Workers AI, no third-party LLM calls)
- **CONNECT** — federated anonymous events across organizations with zero cross-tenant leakage (certified isolation proof)
- **STUDIO** — privacy-native AI session authoring
- **LEARN** & **SOVEREIGN+** — LMS embed rails and regional residency for regulated buyers
- **REACTIONS** — high-throughput ephemeral reactions at the edge

**Spatial sessions (beta):** We're piloting an optional **XR beta** for design partners. It is feature-flagged, opt-in, and never required for voting — standard 2D flows remain the certified path.

v6.x enters a **maintenance window** through 2029-11-03; no breaking changes at upgrade. See `/api/platform/v6-sunset` for dates.
