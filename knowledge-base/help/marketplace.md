# Template and Plugin Marketplace

The Qesto Marketplace is where you find ready-made **session templates** and **plugins**, and
where creators can **publish** their own.

## Browsing and using listings

1. Open the Marketplace from your dashboard.
2. Browse public listings — templates (pre-built question sets and session formats) and
   plugins (add-on capabilities).
3. Use a template to start a new session pre-filled with its questions, then customize it in
   the session wizard.

## Publishing your own listing

If you've built a template or plugin worth sharing, you can list it:

1. Create a listing (template or plugin) from your team's marketplace area.
2. Choose **free** or a **paid** price tier.
3. Publish — once live, it appears to other Qesto users.

### Paid listings and payouts

Paid listings are sold through Qesto's billing, with payouts handled via **Stripe Connect**:

- Purchases are charged securely through Stripe.
- Your earnings accrue to a payout ledger and are paid out to your connected Stripe account.
- You'll need to complete Stripe Connect onboarding before you can receive payouts.

## Which plan do I need?

Anyone can browse and use public listings. Publishing — especially paid listings and payouts —
is part of Qesto's paid plans. See [Pricing and Plans](billing.md) or
[qesto.cc/pricing](https://qesto.cc/pricing) for current details, and the
[Templates and AI](templates-and-ai.md) guide for working with templates in a session.
