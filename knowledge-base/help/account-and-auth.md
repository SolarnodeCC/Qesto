# Account and Sign-In

## Creating an Account

Hosts need a Qesto account to create and run sessions. Participants usually do **not** need an account — they join via link or code.

### Sign up

1. Go to [qesto.cc](https://qesto.cc) and click **Sign up**
2. Enter your email and name, and choose a password (minimum 8 characters)
3. You are logged in and taken to the dashboard

Alternatively, use **Magic link** on the login page: enter your email and click the link we send you — no password required.

## Signing In

Qesto supports two sign-in methods:

### Magic link (recommended)

1. Open the login page → **Magic link** tab
2. Enter your email
3. Check your inbox for an email from Qesto
4. Click the link — you are signed in automatically

Links expire after a short time for security. Request a new one if yours expired.

### Email and password

1. Open the login page → **Log in** tab
2. Enter email and password
3. Click **Log in**

Forgot your password? Use **Reset password** on the login tab. We email you a reset link.

## I Didn't Receive the Magic Link

Try these steps:

1. **Check spam/junk** — automated emails sometimes land there
2. **Wait 2–3 minutes** — delivery can be delayed
3. **Confirm the email address** — typos are the most common cause
4. **Try again** — request a fresh link (old links expire)
5. **Use password login** if you already set a password

Still stuck? Email support@qesto.cc with the address you used. We respond within 2 hours.

## Personal Settings

Open **Settings** from the sidebar footer (`/settings`):

- **Email** — view the address on your account
- **Language** — choose EN, NL, ES, DE, or FR (applies across the app)
- **List density** — compact or comfortable dashboard lists
- **Billing** — upgrade, manage payment method, download invoices (Signal+)

Settings is for **your account**. Team workspace settings (members, SAML) live under **Teams → [team name] → Settings**.

## Signing Out

Click your profile menu → **Sign out**. Your sessions and data remain saved.

## Account vs Participant

| Role | Account needed? |
|------|-----------------|
| Host (create/run sessions) | Yes |
| Team member (collaborate) | Yes |
| Participant (vote in a session) | No — join via link or code |

## Questions?

**Q: Can I use the same email on multiple devices?**
A: Yes. Sign in on any supported browser; your sessions sync to your account.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Contact support@qesto.cc — we can update it for you.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Email support@qesto.cc from your account email. We process deletion requests under our privacy policy (typically within 30 days).
