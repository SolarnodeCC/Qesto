# Getting Started with Qesto

Qesto is a real-time interactive session platform that lets you run live polls, rankings, and team activities with instant feedback and AI-powered insights.

## Your First Session

1. **Sign up** at qesto.cc with your email
2. **Create a team** to organize your sessions
3. **Start a session** and invite participants
4. **Add questions** (polls, rankings, open-ended, and more)
5. **Go live** and watch responses come in real-time
6. **Analyze results** with AI-powered theme extraction

## Key Concepts

- **Session**: A live or draft interactive event with participants
- **Question**: A poll, ranking, consent vote, or open question
- **Energizer**: A fun warm-up activity before the main questions
- **Insights**: AI-extracted themes and trends from responses

## What You Can Do

- Run live sessions with unlimited participants
- Choose from 9 question types
- See real-time response breakdowns
- Export results as CSV
- Get AI-powered insights on closed sessions

Start with a small team meeting or large all-hands to see Qesto in action!

## Creating and Running Your First Session

### Step 1: Create a Session

1. Log in to your Qesto account
2. Click **"New Session"** in the dashboard
3. Enter a session title (e.g., "Q1 Planning Meeting")
4. Select your team
5. Click **"Create"**

Your session is now in **DRAFT** mode. You have time to configure everything before going live.

### Step 2: Add Questions

1. Click **"Add Question"**
2. Choose a question type:
   - **Poll**: Multiple choice with instant voting
   - **Ranking**: Rank items in order
   - **Consent**: Agreement scale (Strongly Disagree → Strongly Agree)
   - **Open**: Free-text responses
   - **Likert**: 1-5 scale survey
   - And more...
3. Write your question and options
4. Click **"Save"**

### Step 3: Add Energizers (Optional)

Warm up your audience with a fun activity before questions start.

1. Click **"Add Energizer"**
2. Choose: Quick Finger, Team Quiz, or Word Cloud
3. Set it up and save

### Step 4: Go Live

1. Click **"Start"** to open the session
2. Share the session link with participants
3. Watch responses come in real-time
4. Click **"Next"** to move between questions

### Step 5: Close and Analyze

1. Click **"Close"** when done
2. See response summaries immediately
3. For Chorus plan: Get AI insights on open responses

That's it! You've run your first session.

## Understanding Question Types

Qesto offers 9 question types to capture different types of feedback:

### Poll (Multiple Choice)

Ask a question with 2-5 options. Participants select one answer.

**Best for**: Decisions, preferences, quick surveys
**Example**: "Which feature should we build next?"

### Ranking

Participants order items from most to least preferred.

**Best for**: Prioritization, preference comparison
**Example**: "Rank these features by importance"

### Consent Vote

Use a 5-point scale (Strongly Disagree to Strongly Agree) to gauge alignment.

**Best for**: Agreement testing, consensus building
**Example**: "I understand the new strategy"

### Open (Free Text)

Participants write in their own response.

**Best for**: Ideas, feedback, open discussion
**Example**: "What's your biggest challenge this quarter?"

### Likert Scale

Rate agreement on a 5-point scale (1-5 or 1-7).

**Best for**: Surveys, satisfaction, sentiment
**Example**: "Rate your satisfaction with our service"

### Upvote

Participants upvote their favorite options (can vote multiple times).

**Best for**: Ideation, popular items
**Example**: "Upvote your top 3 ideas"

### Word Cloud

Participants submit words; size reflects frequency.

**Best for**: Quick brainstorming, tagcloud-style feedback
**Example**: "What word describes our culture?"

### Slider

Participants adjust a numeric slider (0-100 or custom range).

**Best for**: Quantitative ratings, benchmarking
**Example**: "How confident are you in this goal? (0-100)"

### Multi-Select

Participants can select multiple options (unlike Poll).

**Best for**: Multiple choice with many answers
**Example**: "Which topics interest you?"

Each type generates different insights—use the one that best matches your question!

## Inviting Participants and Managing Attendance

### Sharing Your Session

Once you start a session, you get a **session link** to share with participants.

#### Ways to Share

1. **Copy Link**: Click the session link icon to copy to clipboard
2. **Email**: Send via your email client
3. **Slack/Teams**: Paste the link in your team chat
4. **QR Code**: Scan with a mobile device
5. **Project URL**: Share the custom URL (if enabled)

### Participant Anonymity

Qesto supports both identified and anonymous responses:

- **Identified**: Participants see their name when voting (great for team transparency)
- **Anonymous**: Responses are unattributed (great for sensitive feedback)

You can toggle anonymity in session settings before starting.

### Tracking Attendance

During a live session, you can see:

- **Participant count**: Live counter of who's in the session
- **Response rate**: How many have answered each question
- **Dropout trends**: If participants leave after a question

### Inviting After Starting

You can invite more people even after the session has started:

1. Click **"Invite"** button
2. Copy or share the session link
3. New participants join immediately

### Session Limits

- **Pulse plan**: Up to 50 participants per session
- **Signal plan**: Up to 500 participants
- **Chorus plan**: Up to 5,000 participants

If you hit your limit, upgrade your plan or split into multiple sessions.
